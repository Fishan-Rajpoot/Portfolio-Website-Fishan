My Role

In Recycling Center Simulator, I worked as the Programmer, responsible for developing the complete game using the Unity engine and my programming skills. I collaborated with a skilled team of creative professionals to bring the game to life:

  • Implemented and optimized 3D assets provided by the 3D Artist, including environments, machines, and characters.
  • Integrated UI designs from the UI Artist to ensure smooth user interactions.
  • Added audio effects and ambiance provided by the Sound Artist to enhance realism and feedback.
  • Translated gameplay mechanics and features from the Game Designer into fully functional systems.

I was responsible for building and coding core systems such as deal generation, trash collection, sorting machine mechanics, material transformation workflows (e.g., smelting, crushing, pressing), and the resource selling system. Additionally, I handled progression logic, optimization, and bug fixing to deliver a polished and engaging simulation experience.
