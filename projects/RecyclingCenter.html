Recycle Center Simulator

Game Description

Step into the world of Recycle Center Simulator, a 3D resource management game built with Unity. Take on the role of a recycling center manager, tasked with collecting trash from abandoned locations and transforming it into valuable materials.

Use specialized machines to sort, smelt, crush, press, and break down various types of waste. Organize your factory, upgrade equipment, and sell processed materials for profit while contributing to a cleaner environment.
